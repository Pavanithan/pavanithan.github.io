SUPPORTING DOCUMENTS

Whether you are applying online or submitting a physical copy of your application, please include the following documents alongside your application. You can choose to email it or mail/courier the physical copies to the respective institutions below:

  • HELP University/HELP Academy applicants: application@help.edu.my
  • Application Fee (non-refundable) of RM300 (Undergraduate) or RM600 (Postgraduate) and proof of payment (e.g. bank in slip)
  • One (1) set of certified photocopies of all academic certificates
  • Two (2) passport-size photographs
  • A photocopy of your identity card
  • A copy of your sponsor letter
  • A certified copy of your previous qualification to claim for exemption
